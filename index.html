Rules and Guidelines

  • All students of grades 6-11 of Ananda College are eligible for this competition.
  • You can only submit one creation per category. However, you are allowed to participate in more than one category.
  • You can use any software of your choice.
  • You are allowed to use both Sinhala and English languages for your creations. However, using both languages is not required.
  • You are allowed to use images from the internet. However, plagiarizing a design may result in disqualification.

Post Design

  • You must take a screenshot during your creation process for submission purposes.
  • The size of the poster should be 1080x1080 px.

Wishing Card Design

  • You must take a screenshot during your creation process for submission purposes.
  • You're free to use any size and orientation for your wishing card design.

Submission

  • You should submit the final creation in .PNG or .JPG format and a screenshot taken during the creation process.
  • To submit your entry, please use the provided Google Form. Prior registration is not required.
  • Submissions in the improper format and submissions after the deadline may not be accepted.
